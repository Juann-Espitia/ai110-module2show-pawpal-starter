Title: Daily Pet Care Routines
Source: PawPal+ curated routine notes

- Consistent feeding, water refresh, exercise, and observation are the foundation of routine pet care.
- Dogs usually benefit from predictable walk windows and a calm cooldown period after exercise.
- Cats often do better with steady litter box checks, hydration support, and short enrichment sessions.
- When owners are busy, converting care advice into a scheduled task lowers the chance that it gets skipped.
- A planner can support follow-through, but it should not replace veterinary care for illness or emergencies.
