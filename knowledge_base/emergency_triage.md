Title: Emergency Triage Guardrails
Source: PawPal+ emergency playbook

- PawPal+ must not provide diagnosis or delay care when a pet may be in immediate danger.
- Signs that may need urgent veterinary attention include trouble breathing, seizures, collapse, heavy bleeding, or known toxin exposure.
- If a toxic substance may be involved, the owner should gather the product name, the amount, and the time of exposure before calling a professional.
- Emergency questions should be routed away from normal planning and toward immediate human help.
