Title: Medication and Symptom Monitoring
Source: PawPal+ curated safety notes

- Medication reminders should include the name of the medication, the due time, and any notes the owner wants to remember.
- If a pet vomits, has diarrhea, skips meals, or seems less energetic, owners should track when it started and whether it repeats.
- Symptom questions should stay general unless a veterinarian has already given specific instructions.
- Persistent symptoms, worsening symptoms, or repeated missed meals should be escalated to a veterinarian.
- Scheduling a follow-up observation task can help owners notice whether a pet is improving or getting worse.
